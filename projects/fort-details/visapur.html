Visapur Fort

Visapur Fort is a hill fort located near Lonavala in the Pune district. It stands tall adjacent to Lohagad Fort and is popular for its scenic trekking routes and rich history.

Historical Significance

Built in the early 18th century by Balaji Vishwanath, the first Peshwa of the Maratha Empire, Visapur Fort served as an important military post. The fort was later captured and rebuilt by the British during their rule.

Trekking and Accessibility

The trek to Visapur Fort is moderately difficult and often combined with a visit to Lohagad Fort. The route offers beautiful views of the Sahyadri mountain range, especially during the monsoon season.

Key Attractions

  • Ruins of the old fortifications and bastions
  • Water cisterns that retain rainwater
  • Scenic viewpoints overlooking the Western Ghats
  • Proximity to Lohagad Fort for combined trekking

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Visapur Fort is from June to September during the monsoon when the landscape is lush green and the weather is cool.
