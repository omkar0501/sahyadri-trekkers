Shivneri Fort

Shivneri Fort, located about 60 km from Pune, is the birthplace of the great Maratha king, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The fort is a symbol of Maratha pride and history.

Historical Significance

Shivneri played a vital role during the Maratha Empire as a strategic fortification. It was the childhood home of Shivaji Maharaj, making it a place of immense historical importance.

Trekking and Accessibility

The fort is accessible by a moderate trek starting from Junnar village. The trek includes steep climbs, rock-cut steps, and scenic views of the surrounding Sahyadri hills.

Key Attractions

  • The birthplace of Shivaji Maharaj
  • Ancient water tanks and granaries
  • Beautiful bastions and gates
  • Panoramic views of the Sahyadri ranges

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Shivneri Fort is from October to February when the weather is pleasant and ideal for trekking.
