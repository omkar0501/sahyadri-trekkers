Rajgad Fort Trek

Our Experience

Rajgad Fort, the "King of Forts," was the capital of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj's empire for almost 26 years. Trekking here is a unique experience full of history, nature, and breathtaking views.

The trek offers a moderately challenging trail with well-maintained paths. We started early and enjoyed the cool breeze along the way. The fort's vast ramparts and majestic architecture are awe-inspiring.

This trek suits intermediate trekkers and requires basic fitness. We recommend carrying water, snacks, and proper trekking shoes for a comfortable journey.

About the Fort

Rajgad Fort is historically vital as the capital fort of the Maratha Empire. Situated about 40 km from Pune, it stands 1350 meters above sea level and is famous for its strategic location and impressive fortifications.

Trip Duration

1 Day

How to Go (Travel)

Travel by bus from Pune. Pickup points:
• Swargate
• Hadapsar
• Saswad

Food & Stay

Local eateries are available near the base village. There are no formal stay arrangements inside the fort, but tents can be pitched with prior permission.

Things to Explore

  • Maha Darwaja (Main gate)
  • Padmavati Machi
  • Samadhi of Tanaji Malusare
  • Water cisterns and ancient ruins

Trek Difficulty

This trek is moderate with some steep sections. It is suitable for trekkers with basic to intermediate fitness levels.
